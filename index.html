About us

Hinduism is one of the world’s oldest religions,
originating in India over 4,000 years ago.
It is not based on a single founder or book
but on a collection of spiritual traditions,
philosophies, and rituals. Hindus believe in one Supreme Reality called Brahman,
who is present in all things and expressed through many deities
such as Vishnu, Shiva, Durga, Lakshmi, and Krishna.
The religion teaches key principles like karma (the law of cause and effect),
dharma (moral duty), reincarnation (rebirth after death),
and moksha (liberation from the cycle of birth and death).
Sacred texts include the Vedas, Upanishads, Bhagavad Gita, Ramayana,
and Mahabharata. Worship (known as puja) often takes place in homes and temples,
using offerings of flowers, incense, and food.
Hinduism promotes peace, respect for all living beings,
and living a righteous life in harmony with nature.
